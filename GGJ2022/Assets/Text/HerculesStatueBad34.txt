Hercules started the fight and fought hard with all 9 heads, but for every head that he cut, two others grew in its place. The serpent coiled itself around Hercules' leg, pinning him to the swap. The battle was intense, and too much for Hercules to win all by himself. 

He asked for Iolaus' help to get rid of the respawning heads, because it was an impossible task to do alone. Even for a demi-god like Hercules. Iolaus helped burning the severed heads, but there was still one left. The Immortal one. Luckily, they had a sword that was given to them by the gods, and they got the job done. But at what cost?
