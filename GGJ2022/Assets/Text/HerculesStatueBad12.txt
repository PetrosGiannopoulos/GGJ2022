Everyone thought Hercules was not going to make it. And how could he? The monster has 9 heads and has a poison breath! Everyone who tried to defeat the beast left his bones in that swamp. This is an impossible task for anyone, Hercules must be crazy for going against the odds. 

Yet there he is, along with some other poor soul, Iolaus, trying to be heroes in a world of monsters. And this particular monster is one of the worst.
